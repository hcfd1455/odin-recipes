Vanilla Milkshakes without Ice Cream

Cook's Notes:

This milkshake without ice cream is my favorite milkshake recipe of all time! Super-quick, easy, and tasty!

Ingredients

  • 12 ice cubes
  • 2 cups milk
  • Â¾ cup white sugar
  • 1 dash vanilla extract, or to taste

Directions

  1. Place ice, milk, sugar, and vanilla extract into a blender; blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into glasses and serve.
